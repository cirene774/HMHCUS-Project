What is Self-Care

Self-Care is a term that is used to take care of yourself. This includes taking care of your health whether that is mental, phyiscal, or emotional health. If you do not take care of your mental health, than it lead to potential problems in the future.

Examples of Self- Care

    Taking Warm Baths or Showers
    Exercising
    Eating Healthy
    Seeking A Therapist
    Getting A Good Amount of Sleep and Rest
